About the Book

“Novo Ropstvo” is a literary response to the harsh working conditions in modern warehouses and factories. Through the eyes of the worker, the book reveals invisible systems of control, exhaustion, and the loss of dignity.

It raises the question: Is today’s worker truly free — or merely a statistic, a resource to be used up?
